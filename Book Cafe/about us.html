Our facilities

We love books, new and used and everything related to stories. We hope you come and spend some time with us, have a coffee. Sit on a couch, pick up your drink and open your book and enter a different land.
  • When you enter a book café, you will be able to experience value-for-time amidst modern as well as comfortable settings.
  • As a customer you will also be able to browse through any of the several hundred local as well as foreign books, magazines and newspapers which will be available there. It is in a book café where you will be able to refuel yourself while getting lost in the City of Books.
  • You can also get to drink and savor freshly made organic house-roasted coffee while you browse yourself in books from their vast store of shelves.
  • Those of you wish to do their reading online will be able to plug in and reap the benefits of the café's free WI-fi.
  • You will also be able to get the facilities of free Internet access, electrical power-points, easy street-side parking exactly in front of the café.

    • So by now you would have understood the meaning, advantages and the concept behind a book café.
